About

I love what I do! My average day? Not so average. I am fortunate to work for an agency that supports more platforms and frameworks than I can count. Every day is new challenge. I am called to develop and support custom web applications for a diverse client portfolio - Artists, Educational Institutions, Business Professionals and Ecommerce Marketplaces just to name a few. My motivation is to inspire confidence from my Project Management and Sales teams. When the company makes a promise to our valued customers I deliver every time.

Away from work I am a committed husband. My spouse has blessed me with two amazing kids. My primary role is the support the various paths in their lives. While I love my current position my wife’s career has presented her with an opportunity to relocate. My new mission is to find a work-home where I can be challenged and continue to deliver high quality products.

Please take a moment to review my qualifications. I believe my skill sets as a full stack developer will help an organization further deliver on commitments.

My interview availability is flexible.
